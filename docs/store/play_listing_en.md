# Google Play Listing Copy (EN) - PROJECT GTG

## App name
- PROJECT GTG

## Short description
- GTG tracker for push-ups, pull-ups, and dips with quiet reminders.

## Full description
PROJECT GTG is a simple GTG (Grease the Groove) companion for **push-ups, pull-ups, and dips**.

Instead of chasing “perfect workouts”, it helps you stay consistent with **small sets, done often**:
- Log a quick set in seconds
- See daily/weekly/monthly totals per exercise
- Track consistency on a monthly calendar heatmap
- Optional local reminders with quiet hours and weekend skip

## Ads
- The app is free to use.
- Banner ads may appear at the bottom of Settings and the All Logs screen.

## Permissions
- Notifications: requested only when you enable reminders (no forced prompt on first launch).
- Network: may be used to load ads.

## Privacy
- Workout logs/settings are stored locally on your device (no backend).
- Google AdMob may process some device/app data for ad delivery and fraud prevention.
